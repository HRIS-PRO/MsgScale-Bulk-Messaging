# 📖 MsgScale Enterprise - User Manual

Welcome to the **MsgScale Enterprise** platform. This manual provides a comprehensive guide to navigating and managing your bulk messaging operations with speed, precision, and security.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. Authentication
*   **Sign In**: Access the platform using your corporate email and password.
*   **Security**: Your session is protected by enterprise-grade encryption. If you remain inactive for more than 30 minutes, you will be automatically logged out to protect your data.

### 2. Workspace Selection
Upon logging in, you will be presented with the **Workspace Selection** screen.
*   **Choose Organization**: Select the business entity you want to manage.
*   **Create New**: If you have `Admin` or `Manager` privileges, you can create a new workspace using the "Create New Workspace" button.
*   **Logging Out**: If you need to switch accounts or end your session from this screen, use the **Logout** button in the top-right corner.

---

## 🛠 Core Features

### 🏢 Workspace Management
Each workspace is an isolated environment for your campaigns and contacts.
*   **Settings**: Update workspace branding (Logo) and title in the **Settings > General** tab.
*   **Team Management**: Invite team members, assign roles (Admin, Manager, Editor, User), and manage access permissions from the **Settings > Team** tab.
*   **Deletion**: Deleting a workspace is permanent and removes all associated campaigns, contacts, and logs. This requires confirmation of the workspace name.

### 👥 Contacts & Audience
*   **Database**: Centralized storage for your audience.
*   **Groups**: Create segments (e.g., "VIP Customers", "Q4 Leads") to target specific campaign audiences.
*   **Integrations**: Connect external CRM sources (Salesforce, HubSpot) or use webhooks to sync data in real-time via the **Contact Data Integration** dashboard.

### 📣 Campaign Operations
*   **Wizard**: Use the multi-step wizard to compose, test, and launch SMS and Email campaigns.
*   **Templates**: Manage reusable message templates with dynamic placeholders for personalization.
*   **Reports**: Track delivery rates, click-throughs, and engagement metrics in real-time.

## 🔒 Security & Best Practices

*   **Sign Out Properly**: Always use the **Logout** button in the sidebar or the workspace selection page to end your session securely.
*   **Permissions**: Be mindful when assigning `Admin` roles, as they have the power to delete workspaces and manage billing.
*   **Integration Secrets**: Keep your API keys and webhook signing secrets private. Never share them in screenshots or public channels.

## ❓ Troubleshooting

### Why am I seeing a 404 error?
If you encounter a "404 Not Found" page while navigating:
*   Ensure you are accessing the app through the official domain.
*   Do not manually edit the URL in the browser address bar.
*   If the issue persists specifically during logout, contact technical support to ensure the deployment routing is up to date.
